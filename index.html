About the Book

The book "Edge-Enabled 6G Networking: Foundations, Technologies, and Applications" is an edited book to be published by Springer Nature. Edge-enabled 6G Networking integrates edge, fog, and cloud computing paradigms with cellular networks to enable faster and more reliable communication. The book will provide a comprehensive overview of Edge-enabled 6G Networking, including its foundations, architectures, applications, and algorithms, to address the growing interest and demand for this technology.

Call for Chapters

Submission Guidelines

All chapters must be original and not simultaneously submitted to any other journal, conference, or book. The list of topics and important dates are mentioned below. The latex format for the chapter is available here. The chapter abstract with title, author's names, and affiliation should be submitted to edge6g.book@gmail.com. The chapter submission should NOT include any copyrighted materials. All papers will be reviewed and evaluated by independent experts and selected based on their originality, merit, and relevance to the topics. The contents of the chapters will be checked for similarity using plagiarism-checking software. Further instructions will be given for full-chapter submission.
